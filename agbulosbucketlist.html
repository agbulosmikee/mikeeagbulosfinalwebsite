FOOD

My Food Bucket List

  • Try Michelin-star dining
  • Eat at a street food market in Asia
  • Taste authentic pizza in Italy
  • Cook a meal from every culture
  • Try an exotic fruit

Food & Drinks: Why I Want to Explore the World Through Food

Food is more than just something to eat—it’s an experience, a gateway to different cultures, and a way to connect with people. I want to explore the world through its flavors, from the artistry of Michelin-star dining to the authenticity of street food markets in Asia. There’s something magical about eating a dish that has been passed down for generations, prepared with passion and tradition. Trying authentic pizza in Italy, for example, isn’t just about the food itself; it’s about experiencing the culture and history behind it. I also want to challenge myself by cooking a meal from every culture, learning about unique ingredients and techniques that define different cuisines. Tasting exotic fruits is another adventure I look forward to—fruits like durian, mangosteen, or rambutan offer flavors and textures completely different from what I’m used to. Food isn’t just about satisfying hunger; it’s about discovery, connection, and embracing the diversity of the world.

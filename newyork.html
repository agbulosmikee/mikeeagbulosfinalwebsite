How to Get There

By Air

JFK, LaGuardia (LGA), and Newark (EWR) serve international and domestic flights.

By Train

Amtrak provides rail service to Penn Station from major U.S. cities.

By Bus

Greyhound and Megabus offer affordable bus routes to NYC.

By Car

Driving into NYC is possible, but traffic and parking can be challenging.

By Ferry

NYC Ferry connects boroughs like Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Staten Island.

Local Transport

The NYC subway is the fastest way to get around, along with taxis and Uber.

How to Get There

By Air

Auckland Airport (AKL) is the main international gateway, with flights to Wellington (WLG) and Christchurch (CHC).

By Train

Scenic trains like the TranzAlpine and Northern Explorer offer breathtaking views.

By Bus

InterCity buses provide an affordable way to travel between cities and tourist destinations.

By Car

Renting a car or campervan lets you explore at your own pace. Roads are well-maintained for scenic drives.

By Ferry

The Interislander ferry connects the North and South Islands via the Cook Strait.

Local Transport

Cities have buses, trains, and ride-sharing options like Uber for easy transportation.
